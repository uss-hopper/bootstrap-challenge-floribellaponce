As Fin and Jake from Adventure Time would say, Welcome to the House of Shmowzow pups.

Meet the amazing spectacular beautiful magnificent grand wonderful pups who live in the House of Shmowzow pups. The House of Shmowzow is a place where unwanted dogs can be taken to live. A house that would not only care for the pups but also love them unconditionally.

The House of Shmowzow pups currently consists of 4 lovely pups but has contained more before and will contain more in the future.


Candy is a 14 year old female Chow Chow. She was found by the ABQ Westside Animal Shelter roaming on the streets at 3 months. She was adopted a few days later but was not wanted by the new owner. She was them re-homed to the House of Shmowzow pups. Candy has epilepsy but that does not slow her down. She enjoys playing with lasers and likes to nap outside in the winter.

Snow is a 10 year old male Pitbull/Australian Cattle Dog mix. Snow was re-homed to the House of Shmowzow Pups at 6 months due to his owner no longer wanting him. He was known as an aggressive but now his favorite thing to do is bathe humans with kisses. He enjoys playing fetch but mostly enjoys going on long walks.

Yang is a 4 year old female Pomeranian/Chihuahua mix. She was found by the House of Shmowzow Pups on the streets at 2 months and was not reported missing. She has a heart murmur that prevents her from going on long walks but that doesn't stop her from running crazy around the house. She likes to be hugged and walked without a leash.

Lucifer is a 2 year old male Rottweiler/Labrador mix. He was adopted from the ABQ Westside Animal Shelter at 6 months by the House of Shmowzow Pups. He had not been adopted due to his aggression but is now one of the sweetest dogs. He has energy that could power up a house. He loves human attention, kisses and hugs especially.
